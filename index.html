Collection of Scents

Smell is usually seen as the least important of the 5 human senses, it's often forgotten or neglected.
But I've always been intrigued by different smells. They are personal, emotional and powerful.
Hence this collection explores the 11 scents that I encounter in subjective, imaginative, abstract and expressive ways.
